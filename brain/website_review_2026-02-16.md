# Philip D'Agostino Website Review
**Date:** February 16, 2026
**Reviewer:** Robothor
**Original URL:** https://philipdagostino.com

---

## Executive Summary

The domain **philipdagostino.com** is currently **unavailable** (DNS error: NXDOMAIN). Through the Wayback Machine, I discovered the site has a complex history:

1. **2011-2015**: Legitimate music portfolio site for Philip D'Agostino (Philadelphia bassist/musician)
2. **2018**: Domain listed for sale on Sedo ($499 USD)
3. **2021-2022**: Domain compromised with Vietnamese gambling/casino spam content
4. **2022-Present**: Domain offline/DNS not resolving

**Key Finding:** This was Philip's **music career website**, not a professional/business site. It predates his current work in AI/pharmaceutical technology by many years.

## Domain Strategy Options

### Option 1: Reacquire philipdagostino.com

- Cons: May be expensive, has been compromised/spam-associated

### Option 2: New personal domain
- Options: philipdagostino.net, philipdagostino.io, philipdagostino.ai
- Pros: Clean slate, modern TLD options
- Cons: Less intuitive for people searching

### Option 3: Company-focused domain
